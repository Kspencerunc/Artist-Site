About Kari

Kari Spencer is an artist that was raised in Graham, NC. Throughout College she has maintained residency in the triangle area. She is currently attending grad school and working on furthering her portfolio, with her main mediums being Oil painting and ceramic.

Artistic Themes Include the Following

  • Horror Art

    Personal interest on creating semi-relistic works that intill a sense of fear or discomfort in the viewer. Or enjoyment for the horror-loving viewer.

  • Vision/Eyes

    Due to a personal experience, there is a current obsession on eyes, vision, and creating a sense of unease on the subject.

  • Health

    Both a focus on mental and physical health. With particular interest to how mental health can actually affect physical health.
